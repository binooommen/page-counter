🐔 The Chicken and the Elevator 🚪

Once upon a time, a chicken walked into a fancy hotel and waddled up to the elevator.

The bellboy asked, “What floor?”
The chicken said, “Egg-cellent question. Surprise me!”

The elevator doors closed. Moments later, the elevator dinged open... on the rooftop.

The chicken looked around, nodded thoughtfully, and said, “Cluckin’ worth it.” 🐣
